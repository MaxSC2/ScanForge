# Idea Inbox — ScanForge

## Purpose

Raw storage for ideas.

This is NOT:
- roadmap
- backlog
- current work

---

## How to use

When idea appears:
- write it here
- do NOT implement immediately
- do NOT move to stage without validation

---

## Sections

### Candidate ideas
Ideas that might become backlog items

---

### Not validated
Unclear or experimental ideas

---

### Later maybe
Low priority or long-term ideas

---

## Rules

- no implementation from this file
- no mixing with CURRENT_STAGE
- ideas must be evaluated before moving to BACKLOG

---

## Goal

Prevent:
- context switching
- feature creep
- unfinished projects

Keep ideas without breaking focus
